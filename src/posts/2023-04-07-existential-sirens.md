And so it begins
I become john green.

he helped me see
I have ocd

its a rather tricky variant
it hides in plain sight as unimportant

but my existence,
ah my existence,
it beckons me like the sirens call

why am i here at all?

(ryan has existential ocd. A somewhat rarer form of OCD)
